US Data from 2021-2024

This is the data for the years 2021 to 2024.

Average Temperature Increase:The average temperature in the United States rose from 53.3°F in 2021 to 54.6°F in 2024. This represents an increase of 1.3°F over the four-year period. Urbanization Impact:The impact of urbanization on climate increased from 12.5% in 2021 to 14.5% in 2024, indicating a growing influence of urban heat islands and land-use changes. Globalization Impact:The influence of globalization on climate factors increased from 9.8% in 2021 to 11.1% in 2024, reflecting rising industrialization and transportation effects. CO2 Emissions:Total CO2 emissions increased from 5,120 Mt in 2021 to 5,250 Mt in 2024, contributing to the overall warming trend. Average Precipitation:Average annual precipitation remained relatively stable, with a slight increase from 32.5 inches in 2021 to 33.4 inches in 2024.
